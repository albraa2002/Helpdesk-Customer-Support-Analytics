🎧 Customer Support & Helpdesk Analytics Dashboard

📌 Project Overview
In the SaaS and tech industry, Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) is the primary driver of retention and recurring revenue. This project analyzes a simulated dataset of 5,000 helpdesk tickets over 6 months to evaluate support team performance, track SLA compliance, and identify product bottlenecks.

🚀 Key Business Insights
Product Feedback Loop: Discovered that "Bug Reports" yield the lowest CSAT score (3.92), falling below the 4.2 company target. This highlights a direct correlation between technical bugs, extended resolution times, and customer frustration.
Workload Distribution: "Technical Issues" account for the highest ticket volume (1,517 tickets, 30.3% of total), indicating a need for expanded Tier-2 technical support or better self-service documentation.
SLA & Resolution Efficiency: Analyzed resolution times across priorities using Box Plots, confirming that the support team successfully prioritizes "Urgent" tickets with tightly clustered, minimal resolution hours.
